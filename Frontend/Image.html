CROP PROTECTION

Empowering our Farmers

Why Crop Protection?

Our farmers work hard all day long over the year to yield a healthy batch of crop. However, diseases and pests take a heavy toll on agricultural crops causing severe loss to the farming community. This can be overcome with the help of an efficient disease detection system for crop protection.

We detect the type of disease, insect or pest the crop could have been affected with. Looking at the plant type and farmer's location, required information about the crop and its health is delivered.

How does Crop Protection work?

Primary use of image processing allows us to detect plant health and hence the disease or pest affecting it. The portal then delivers suggested remedies and other measures to improve crop health.
If a farmer is somehow unable to upload crop picture, then the use of diseases' symptoms can be made for recognising the problem.

Additionally, helpline numbers of the nearest agriculture units are provided for further help.

Why develop Crop Protection?

For two very simple reasons:-
1) Helping farmers produce a better yield and hence secure their income at all costs.
2) Lead to judicial use of pesticides and insecticides by avoiding unnecessary spr.
